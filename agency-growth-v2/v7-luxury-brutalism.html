01

What blocks growth

The work is there. The capability is there. But without clarity, packaging, and orchestration, momentum stalls. Here are the eight blockers I see most often.

1
Market readiness
Foundations aren't solid. Push sales before fixing this and you amplify the cracks. Progress doesn't stick.
2
Disconnected teams
Everything feels siloed. Delivery is busy but doesn't feed growth. No shared vision of what you're building.
3
No productised offer
There's no defined programme with fixed outcomes. Prospects struggle to understand what they're saying yes to.
4
Core assets weak
Website, copy, case studies exist but positioning doesn't land hard enough. You feel it yourself.
5
No orchestration
Not enough bandwidth internally to align everything. Momentum depends on luck, not system.
6
Pace mismatch
Progress feels slower than it should. Capability is there but urgency isn't matching velocity.
7
Pipeline blind
No clear pipeline stages. Hard to see what's actually in play. Forecasting feels like guesswork.
8
Senior cost barriers
You need senior thinking but can't justify £120k–£150k for a full-time hire right now.
